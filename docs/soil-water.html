4.2 Experiment details

  1. 2 years experiment.
  2. 2 sites
    1. Lincoln Uni
    2. Ashely Dene
  3. 10 sowing date in both sites.
    1. five sowing date in the first year
    2. five sowing date in second year

Note: The setup helps to compare the seedling and regrowth in the same conditions in Year 2.

  1. 1 cultivar.
  2. 4 reps each site.
  3. 2 soil types
    1. stone free - plant available water capaciy (PAWC) 360mm/2.3m
    2. stony - PAWC 240/2.3m This type is for a grazing trial. probably not a good idea for a starting?
    3. very stony 130mm/2.3m
  4. 22 layers of soil water measurements - 10 cm interval below top 20 cm
    1. top 20 cm was measured by TDR.
    2. remaining 21 layers was meastured by Neutron probe
  5. 1 follow treat in both sites - absolute soil evaporation
